\section{Falsifications Across Voices}
\label{v4-arith:app:B:falsifications}

Nine specific naive claims were falsified by the adversarial protocol:

\begin{enumerate}
\item $\mathcal{V}^{\mathrm{prim}}$ is a VOA --- \textbf{false}
(Polyakov): central charge $=\sum_{p}2=\infty$; $\zeta_{\mathbb{P}}(0)$
has natural boundary.
\item $\bigotimes^{!}_{p}\mathcal{H}^{(p)}$ is defined over a field
--- \textbf{false} (Drinfeld+Kapranov): different $\mathbb{Q}_{p}$
cannot be glued into a single field.
\item BD factorization algebra on $\mathrm{Spec}\,\mathbb{Z}$ ---
\textbf{false} (Costello+Gwilliam): $\mathbb{Z}_{p}\,\widehat{\otimes}_{\mathbb{Z}}\,\mathbb{Z}_{p}=\mathbb{Z}_{p}$,
no $p$-adic diagonal thickening.
\item $T_{n}$ lifts Adams to $GL_{2}$ Hecke ---
\textbf{false} (Kazhdan+Beilinson): explicit
$T_{p}^{2}(a^{(p)}_{-1}|0\rangle)-p\cdot a^{(p)}_{-1}|0\rangle\neq T_{p^{2}}(a^{(p)}_{-1}|0\rangle)$.
\item $\mathcal{V}^{\mathrm{prim}}$ is a Borger $\Lambda$-ring ---
\textbf{false or vacuous} (Gelfand+Borger): Wilkerson congruence
fails on Fock $\mathbb{Z}$-lattice; vacuous over $\mathbb{C}$.
\item Arithmetic Yangian via RTT ---
\textbf{false} (Drinfeld-solo): no $u-v$ across distinct primes,
no meromorphic $1$-form on $\mathrm{Spec}\,\mathbb{Z}$, etc.
\item 4D class-$\mathcal{S}$ gauge boundary ---
\textbf{false} (Gaiotto): Arakelov $H^{0}(\overline{C},K^{k})=0$,
branches vanish.
\item $D$-modules on $\mathrm{Bun}_{GL_{2}}(\mathrm{Spec}\,\mathbb{Z})$
--- \textbf{false} (Bezrukavnikov+Bernstein):
$\mathrm{Bun}_{GL_{2}}(\mathrm{Spec}\,\mathbb{Z})$ is a point
(class number 1).
\item $E_{2}$-chiral-centre enhancement integral ---
\textbf{false} (Kontsevich+Soibelman): Dyer--Lashof mod $p$ /
Kaledin Japanese cocycle obstructs.
\end{enumerate}
